Description

Virus, The Beauty of the Beast is an interactive documentary about viruses and their connections to human designs, based on actual scientific models.

About

The development process

The "interactive documentary" is a completely new format. I tried something similar in The Stranger Loop (a previous work of mine), but that was extremely basic (made in 4 days).

The Science of the three models

The "hexagons and pentagons" model describing Hepatitis, discovered in 1960, is one of the most important models in all of structural biology. It describes the majority of viruses (and since viruses are 90% of living things, it describes the majority of living things). Almost every new discovery regarding virus structure is seen in light of this model. One of the largest virus information databases, viperDB, organizes their data around this model.

The "Islamic art" model that describes Zika virus and HPV is more of a wildcard. It was published extremely recently (2014). It has been discussed, and the mathematics all works out, but there are a few experiments that still need to be done with it. The Islamic art connection is roundabout but very real, and subject of a peer reviewed publication in a mathematical art journal.

The "corner moving" model describing HIV is a new model developed as part of my Master's dissertation. Its interest is mostly mathematical - IT IS NOT LIKELY AT ALL TO LEAD TO A CURE FOR HIV! There are other means of modelling viruses shells like HIV's which have been used. However, it is definitely simpler than any previous model and does enable some new things, so it may turn out to be useful for modelling other viruses. Work on it has not yet been peer-reviewed, but it is known to be compatible with all existing data on virus structure. For more details, contact me!
