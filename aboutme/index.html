Hamish Todd's personal webpage

Hey, I'm Hamish, and I believe that good game design can be used to communicate sophisticated ideas.
  • I made Virus, the Beauty of the Beast, an interactive documentary about viruses [coming very soon!]
  • I made Music of the Spheres, a puzzle game about bouncing bullets and sound
  • Here are some articles of mine about classic games and what they communicate to us: Portal, Castlevania, Tomb Raider, Half Life, Shadow of the Colossus, Super Meat Boy, Super Mario Bros 3

You can email me at moc.liamg@1ddot.hsimah and sponsor my work on patreon.
