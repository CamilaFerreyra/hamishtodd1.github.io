Hamish Todd's personal webpage

Hello! I'm Hamish, and I believe that sophisticated ideas can be communicated clearly and beautifully using carefully-designed interactive systems.
  • I'm making CootVR, a VR program for interpreting electron microscope data
  • I made Virus, the Beauty of the Beast, an interactive documentary about viruses
  • I made Music of the Spheres, a puzzle game about bouncing bullets and sound
  • Here are some articles of mine about classic video games and how they communicate clearly and beautifully: Portal, Castlevania, Tomb Raider, Half Life, Shadow of the Colossus, Super Meat Boy, Super Mario Bros 3
